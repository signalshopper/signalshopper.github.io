Treasury yields are spiking this morning as trade war fears drive investors out of US Treasuries (speculation). The honest thesis is that hedge funds are selling treasuries because the basis trade (the difference between Treasury futures and Treasury bond prices) only works when prices are stable. Some pundits also speculate that “leveraged investors are raising cash” to meet margin calls, and foreign investors may be unwinding US  treasury positions.
