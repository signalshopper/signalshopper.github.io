Bad news is good news, again. Markets are slightly higher premarket as investors await the 2:00 ET release of the Federal Reserve's July FOMC minutes. We assume the massive government jobs adjustment (reducing the employment numbers from April '23 to March '24 by 600k  to 1M or ~50K per month) will provide the Federal Reserve leeway (a recession signal) to lower rates in September. Currently, markets are pricing in a 69.5% chance of a 25-bps rate cut in September and a 30.5% chance of a 50-bps cut.
