Stock index futures look to open lower after warmer-than-expected inflation prints (see below). Bulls will point out that most of the miss was due to higher food and shelter costs, but the Bears will note that Fed Chairman Powell has previously said these categories, along with labor, are a primary concern of the Fed. We find it extraordinary that the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) continues to report exactly 0.10% changes to every important metric, only to revise the numbers more negatively later. A skeptic might think the fix is in.
