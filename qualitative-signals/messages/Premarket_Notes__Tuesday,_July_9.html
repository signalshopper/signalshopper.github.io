"Countries go bankrupt gradually then all at once." Hemingway

Economist Peter St Onge wrote an interesting piece titled 'Will Debt Sink the American Empire.' We highly recommend it (link below). In the piece, St Onge provides examples of countries/empires having sunk under the weight of debt (Rome, France, Spain, Britain, etc.). Historically, the debt bomb resolves itself when "investors stop buying government debt, shutting out governments and leading to massive austerity and soaring inflation as the government retrenches (see Argentina)." The US debt is 134% of GDP, growing $1T every 100 days. Can the expected outcome be stopped? Perhaps, but our spendthrift vote-buying politics will need to change course. (St Onge, Substack)
