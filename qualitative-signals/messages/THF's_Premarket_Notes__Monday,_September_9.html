Friday’s market action left bulls reeling after three days of adverse *economic data implied a looming recession. Risk On: Today, hope springs eternal, and we expect high beta stocks, e.g., AAPL, NVDA, TSLA, etc., to catch bids. However, we remain cautious as the institutions are likely to continue distributing (sell) stocks to hopeful Main Street buyers.

Friday, the US Employment report (includes the public sector) missed expectations by 18K jobs.
