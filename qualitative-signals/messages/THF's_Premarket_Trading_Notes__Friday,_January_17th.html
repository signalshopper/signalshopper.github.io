LA Wildfire Trades?

While this trade is probably one to two quarters away, we may want to consider investing in the beneficiaries of the LA wildfire rebuild (12K+ structures), i.e., building material companies (Builders First Source (BLDR), Boise Cascade (BCC), etc.) and the lumber suppliers (Canfor (CFP), West Fraser Timber (WFG), Weyerhaeuser (WY), etc.).

 

The US lumber market has been soft, making this trade more interesting as demand for building supplies and lumber increases due to the CA wildfire rebuild (+12k structures), tariffs on Canadian lumber, and seasonal purchasing strength (February-March is the peak season), will move these stocks. Today, the CME lumber contracts for March delivery settled at $585 per 1,000 board feet (+7% month/month versus 1.3% last year). We are watching.
