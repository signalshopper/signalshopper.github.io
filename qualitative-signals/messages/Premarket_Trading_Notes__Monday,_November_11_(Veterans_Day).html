Premarket Trading Notes: Monday, November 11 (Veterans Day)

Date: 2024-11-11 08:16:08

Markets

 

Premarket Futures:  DJIA +0.38%  SPX +0.29%  NASDAQ +0.29%  Gold -1.86%  Oil -2.67%  BTC +2.7%

 

There will be a reversion to the mean at some point, but it will not be at the open today as Trump’s growth-focused agenda and declining rates continue to power the indexes into overbought territory.

 

This week, markets are bracing for US inflation data (CPI/Wed, PPI/Thurs) and no less than eleven Federal Reserve speakers.  Earnings releases are slowing down, but we will hear from Home Depot (HD) on Tuesday and learn if customers are still putting off more expensive home improvements.  

 

President Trump promised to make America the “crypto capital of the world.” Digital asset investors are taking him at his word. Bitcoin (BTC, $82,064) is up 19.10% over the last seven days, Ethereum (ETH, $3,176) has risen 28.50%, and Solana (SOL, $216.67) is up 31.70%.  

 

Valuation: The S&P 500 (SPX, 5,595.50) trades at 22.2 times earnings estimates for the next 12 months. That compares with a five-year average of 19.6 times. (FactSet)

 

Market Movers

 

VIX:  15.29 (+0.35) Let the good times roll.

 

Treasuries: The bond market is closed for Veteran’s Day.

  • * Traders have lowered expectations for a 25 bps point rate cut in Dec. to 69% from 80% last week.

 

Currencies:  USD Basket/DXY: 105.609 (+0.66) Above 105 is considered restrictive.

  • * The DXY is at its highest level since July.

 

Precious Metal Futures:  Gold 2,663.60 (-31.50)  Silver 31.20 (-0.251)  Copper 4.277 (-0.029)

  • * Gold and silver are weaker as markets speculate that the Fed will reduce the pace of rate cuts in light of rising inflation expectations if Trump stimulates the economy with tariffs, tax reductions, and deregulation. (Opinion: Bravo Sierra).

 

Energy Futures:  WTI 68.86 (-1.52)  Brent 72.50 (-1.37)  Nat Gas 2.88 (+0.211)  Gasoline RBOB 1.978 (-0.034)

  • * Oil prices fell in Asia overnight as more fiscal stimulus measures from China continue to be underwhelming, and the hurricane in the Gulf of Mexico appears to have a limited impact on US production.
  • * We remind investors that integrated oil companies are very profitable when oil prices are above $60.

 

Crypto:  BTC 82,323 (+19.5% W/W) ETH 3,182 (+28.7% W/W)  SOL 216.52 (+32.2% W/W)  IBIT 46.93 (+$3.27)

 

  • * BTC is up 91% YTD as the Trump crypto rally continues on expectations for a more friendly regulatory environment.
  • * What is SOL? Solana (SOL) is a blockchain designed to host decentralized, scalable applications. In simple terms, SOL, the fourth largest coin ($102.3B market cap), is a faster, less expensive crypto applications development tool than Ethereum.
  • * Trading platform Coinbase (COIN, +16.3%) and bitcoin miners Mara (MARA, +19.4%) and Riot Platforms (RIOT, +13.4%) also had a great week.
