Political news aside, this week is one of the busiest corporate earnings releases of the quarter, including market-moving AAPL, MSFT, AMZN, META, V, KO, and CAT. Market-moving economic data include Wednesday’s PCE data (the Fed’s preferred measure of inflation) and Friday’s April unemployment report. If the PCE data is softer than expected, the market will rally on expectations for a rate cut.
