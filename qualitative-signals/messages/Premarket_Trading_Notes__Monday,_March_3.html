Will Tariff Tuesday be the catalyst for an economic slowdown? We don’t know, but President Trump’s speech to Congress should provide something similar to an economic roadmap. Despite all the gnashing of teeth and apocalyptic pronouncements, we note that tariffs worked for President Coolidge. Trump aside, we will be looking for economic clues when Target (TGT) and Wall Street darling Costco (COST) report later this week, along with AI chip darling Broadcom (AVGO).  

 

Crypto: Short-term crypto sellers are likely regretting recent sales after President Trump announced the creation of a strategic crypto reserve, causing analysts to recommend BTC, ETH, XRP, SOL, and ADA. Our bullish view of cryptocurrency (not meme coins) remains unchanged. MicroStrategy (MSTR, +13%) and Coinbase (COIN, +9%) are moving higher on the news.
