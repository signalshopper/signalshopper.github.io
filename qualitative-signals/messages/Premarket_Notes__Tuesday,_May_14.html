Markets

 

Equity futures were lower after the hotter-than-expected PPI report (recession watch) but stabilized after [suspicious] revisions to the March PPI and PPI Y/Y numbers. We suspect the market is waiting for tomorrow’s CPI numbers to confirm the negative news (see Economic Reports below). Will Fed Chairman J. Powell’s 10:00 ET comments improve the market’s mood? Inquiring minds want to know.

 

Valuation: “With rates normalized and the pop in core PCE to just over 3%, our models indicate that Fed rate cuts will be pushed back further and cause a 2Q-3Q correction for equities. A decline of roughly 10% (500 points) would take the S&P 500 to ~ 4,750 from 5200. Our models show core  PCE in the 2H of 2024 at more than 3% and the Fed’s 2% core PCE more of a pipe dream.” (Stifel) https://www.marketwatch.com/story/the-next-500-points-for-the-s-p-500-are-down-says-stifel-heres-why-7e2a13b9?mod=mw_more_headlines
