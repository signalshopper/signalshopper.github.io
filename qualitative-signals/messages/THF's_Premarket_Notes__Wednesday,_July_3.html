Markets look to open lower after weaker-than-expected employment numbers point to a declining US economy. The ADP report showed fewer private-sector jobs were added than expected in June, while initial claims rose in the ninth week to 1.86M, the highest in over two years. The market-moving June FOMC Minutes will be released at 2:00 ET, after the market closes.

 

Note: US stock exchanges will close at 1:00 ET today in observance of the Fourth of July and reopen Friday.
