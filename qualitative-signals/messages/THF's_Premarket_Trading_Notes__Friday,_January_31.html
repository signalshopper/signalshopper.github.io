Markets look to open higher after last night’s reports from AAPL and INTC. AAPL (+3.4%) provided investors with a bullish 2025 outlook despite awful iPhone sales in China, and INTC (+2.0%) beat quarterly expectations. The annualized PCE Index inflation rate rose to 2.6% from 2.4%, marking the third consecutive monthly rise, while core PCE inflation remained steady at 2.8%, both in line with forecasts.
