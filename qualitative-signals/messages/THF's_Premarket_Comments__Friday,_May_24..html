Markets

 

Yesterday, the markets discarded Nvidia’s great quarter and positive outlook after the bullish PMI data (good economic news) quashed lingering expectations for a rate reduction in September. This morning, stocks look to bounce at the open, although that may change after 9:35 when hawkish Fed Governor Christopher Waller speaks.

 

Have a great Memorial Day weekend!

 

Food for thought: The Dow Theory is a popular tenet of technical analysis that holds if the Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA)  rises above a key level and is accompanied by the Dow Transportation Average (DJT), an uptrend is underway. Unfortunately, the averages have diverged since December, with the DOW rising and the Dow Transports moving sideways.
