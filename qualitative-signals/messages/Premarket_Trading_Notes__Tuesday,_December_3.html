Futures are little changed as investors await the JOLTs job data (10:00 ET) and comments from three Federal Reserve speakers later today (see Economic News). Stocks in Europe are rising after new US restrictions on tech exports to China under the Biden administration proved less severe than initially anticipated (positive for semiconductor companies).

 

Yesterday’s gains were driven by a strong performance in technology stocks, with AAPL (+1%), MSFT (+1.8%), AMZN (+1.4%), META (+3.2%), and AVGO (+2.7%). It’s worth noting that AAPL, the largest company by market cap ($3.62B), announced a partnership with Coinbase (COIN), allowing users to buy crypto via Apple Pay. The addition to Coinbase Onramp will allow the 60M+ US  users of Apple Pay to instantly bring money on-chain, giving Apple a small piece of the recent crypto craze.

 

“Coinbase Onramp takes the hassle out of fiat-to-crypto conversions with lightweight KYC for eligible purchases, free USDC on and off ramping, and access to the most popular payment methods. With Apple Pay, getting on chain only takes seconds.” (https://www.coinbase.com/blog/Fiat-to-crypto-in-seconds-with-Apple-Pay).
