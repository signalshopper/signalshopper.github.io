MicroStrategy (MSTR, $373.43): After a sixfold surge in its stock price this year, Michael Saylor’s bitcoin BTC investment firm MicroStrategy (MSTR) looks like it will join one of the largest exchange-traded funds, the $312B Invesco QQQ ETF by year-end (QQQ, $516.87). QQQ tracks the NASDAQ-100 Index, which tracks the 100 largest non-financial companies listed on the NASDAQ. With daily trading volumes in the tens of billions of dollars, QQQ guarantees passive, permanent capital flows. “For millions of passive investors, owning ETFs like QQQ will provide indirect bitcoin exposure to their portfolios through MSTR’s BTC holdings (~$37B).” Since these passive funds are often buyers at any price, their participation can exert significant upward pressure on the equity’s price. (Source: CoinDesk)
