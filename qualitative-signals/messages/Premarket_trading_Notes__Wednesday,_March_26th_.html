Valuation: Barclays strategists lowered their year-end S&P 500 price target to 5,900 from 6,600. Barclay’s base case is for the US economy to slow but avoid a recession. They lowered their S&P earnings estimate to $262 from $271 on expectations of weaker consumption and slower overall activity. Meh, Barclay’s is looking for a 2% year-end rise in valuation until further notice.
