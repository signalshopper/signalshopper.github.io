Markets

 

It’s a tough day to call the direction of the markets. One, it’s a triple witching day, and two, the SPX’s Relative Strength Index is above 70, and the NDX is 80. Historically, the RSI has been a reliable overbought/oversold signal and an RSI above 70 is considered overbought. We plan to wait until this morning’s economic data is released before we decide, although this morning’s declining treasury yields suggest expectations are for a bullish inflation print.
